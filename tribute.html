NETHAJI SUBHASH CHANDRA BOSE

ABOUT THE LEADER




  • ⇨Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian Nationalist whose patriotism towards India has left a mark in the hearts of many Indians.

  • ⇨He is famously known as the founder of 'Azad Hind Fauj' and his famous slogan is 'Tum Mujhe Khoon Do, Main Tumhe Aazadi Dunga'.

  • ⇨Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose was born on 23 January,1897 in Orissa and he died on 18 August,1945, in a Taiwan hospital after suffering from burn injuries sustained in a plane crash.

  • ⇨For Indian freedom he gave many slogans, some of his famous slogans are 'tum mujhe khoon do, main tumhe aazadi dunga','Jai Hind', and'Delhi Chalo'.

  • ⇨He formed Azad Hind Fauj and made several contributions to India's freedom struggle. He is known for his militant approach that he used to gain independence and for his socialist policies.

  • ⇨He joined the Non-Cooperation Movement which was started by Mahatma Gandhi who made INC a powerful non-violent organization.
